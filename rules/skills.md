# Skills — *The Queen's Revel*

Skills represent trained and studied competencies — distinct from supernatural discipline powers. Like stats, skills are rated 0–5 and serve as **re-test pools**: spend 1 point from a relevant skill to demand a fresh RPS throw.

## How skills work

**Buying skills at character creation.** Spend Skill points (separate from Attribute and Power points) to fill in skill levels. Each point fills one level bubble.

**Structured distribution (MET-style).** Rather than spending freely, use this template for your rank:

| | Sprite | Sidhe | Sovereign |
|---|---|---|---|
| Skills at 4 | 1 | 1 | 1 |
| Skills at 3 | 1 | 2 | 4 |
| Skills at 2 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| Skills at 1 | 2 | 4 | 4 |
| **Total dots** | **13** | **20** | **28** |

You may deviate from the template with Storyteller approval, as long as totals match and no skill exceeds 5.

**Maximum 5 per skill.** No skill may be raised above 5 at creation.

**Re-tests work like stat re-tests.** Declare the relevant skill when demanding a re-test, cross off 1 point. Points spent are gone for the night.

## Talents *(innate — natural gifts, not formally taught)*

| Skill | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Brawl** | unarmed fighting — striking, grappling, raw physical contest |
| **Awareness** | keen natural perception; noticing things without supernatural aid |
| **Empathy** | reading emotions and intentions in others |
| **Intimidation** | threatening and frightening through presence or words |
| **Reveling** | song, dance, and storytelling as natural gifts — the fae arts of celebration |

---

## Skills *(trained — require instruction and practice)*

| Skill | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Bladesmanship** | fighting with melee weapons of any kind |
| **Stealth** | moving unseen and unheard without magical aid |
| **Court Protocol** | knowing proper etiquette and behaviour in fae courts |
| **Wildcraft** | surviving, navigating, and reading signs in the wild |
| **Beastcraft** | non-magical handling, reading, and working with animals |

*Note: Beastcraft covers mundane competence with animals. The Green discipline covers supernatural communion with them. They stack — a Beast court Sidhe with both can speak to animals AND handle them expertly.*

---

## Knowledges *(studied — require time and a teacher)*

| Skill | What it covers |
|---|---|
| **Faerie Lore** | knowledge of fae creatures, magic, the Otherworld, and its rules |
| **Fae Law** | oaths, geisa, bargains, court law, and the weight of spoken promises |
| **Investigation** | deduction, finding clues, reading scenes and motives |
| **Court Politics** | alliances, rivalries, debts, and who holds power in the courts |
| **Heraldry** | genealogies, bloodlines, court histories, and symbols of power |

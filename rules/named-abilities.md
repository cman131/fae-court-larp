# Named Character Abilities — *The Queen's Revel*

---

## Knight Signature Abilities

Each Knight's signature ability is pre-loaded before character creation begins. It does not cost stat points and **cannot be purchased for additional uses** — the Knight has exactly 1 use for the evening. Knights spend their stat points on stats and standard ability pool purchases as normal.

- **Sun's Edge** *(Iron)* — Win a Might challenge without RPS. *Barghest only.*
- **Lake's Veil** *(Wyrd)* — Vanish from a scene for up to 5 real minutes; return anywhere in the play space. *Mélusine only.*
- **Wind's Lament** *(Wyrd)* — Compel one direct, truthful answer to one question. *Baobhan Sith only.*

## Vortigern's Abilities

> Storyteller reference only. Do not share with other players before the reveal.

Oberon's player builds using a standard character sheet and spends stat points freely. The abilities below are pre-made and activate only when Oberon becomes Vortigern at the storyteller's cue. They are not purchased and do not exist before the reveal.

*See [storyteller/vortigern-reveal.md](../storyteller/vortigern-reveal.md) for the full reveal procedure.*

- **Vortigern's Heart** — Fires automatically on the reveal. Gain +5 stat points distributed freely across any stats. All oaths Oberon swore are retroactively void.
- **Tyrant's Word** *(Wyrd)* — Passive for the rest of the evening: cannot be lied to.
- **The Long Hunger** *(Iron)* — Each character you defeat in a challenge after the reveal: choose one of their purchased abilities. You may use that ability once tonight as if you had bought a use of it.

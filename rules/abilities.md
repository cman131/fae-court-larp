# Abilities — *The Queen's Revel*

Abilities bend the rules in ways ordinary stat challenges cannot. Activating an ability costs **1 action point** from your pool. There are no per-scene or per-evening limits — your action point supply is the only constraint.

When you use an ability, narrate it. The mechanical effect happens regardless, but the storytelling is what the rest of the room remembers.

Players choose abilities at character creation from two pools: **Universal** and **Court**.

## Universal Abilities

Anyone may take from this pool.

- **Glamour-Strike** *(Court — Glamour)* — Force the opponent to lose an RPS challenge without throwing.
- **Iron Word** *(Court — Presence)* — When you swear an oath aloud, you cannot break it without a Wyrd-Lore conflict at 5+; in return, you cannot be magically compelled this scene.
- **Whispered Tongue** — Pass a short message silently to one person you can see. No stat required.
- **Hearth's Mercy** — You are unaffected by one ability just used against you this scene.
- **Trueseeing** *(Wyrd — Sight)* — Demand a yes/no question be answered honestly.

## Court of Storms

*(Aurora's court)*

- **Lure of the Revel** *(Court — Glamour)* — Draw any one character into a private conversation; they cannot disengage for 3 minutes without spending a Glamour point.
- **Stormwalk** *(Iron — Grace)* — Pass any one social barrier (a closed circle, a forbidden room) without challenge.
- **Song of Aurora** *(Wyrd — Lore)* — While you sing, dance, or perform, any character within earshot must answer one personal question truthfully when you stop.

---

## Court of Beasts

*(Spriggan's court)*

- **Beast Form** *(Iron — Mettle)* — When you would lose a physical conflict, force a tie instead.
- **Earth-Sense** *(Wyrd — Sight)* — Detect when anyone in your line of sight is lying about a location, a movement, or a meeting.
- **Old Grudge** *(Court — Presence)* — Publicly name a wrong done to your clan. The accused must respond honestly or spend a Presence point to deflect.

---

## Court of Mirrors

*(Mab's court)*

- **Maskwear** *(Court — Guile)* — Briefly impersonate another character's voice or manner. Convince one character of one small lie.
- **Boggart Bargain** *(Court — Guile)* — Offer a secret-for-secret trade. If accepted, both parties must trade truthfully.
- **Hall of Mirrors** *(Iron — Grace)* — When attacked, redirect the attack to another character within 3 paces.

---

## Knight Signature Abilities

Each Knight receives their signature ability in addition to their three picks.

- **Sun's Edge** *(Iron — Might)* — Win a Might challenge without RPS. Barghest only.
- **Lake's Veil** *(Wyrd — Craft)* — Vanish from a scene for up to 5 real minutes; return anywhere in the play space. Mélusine only.
- **Wind's Lament** *(Wyrd — Lore)* — Compel one direct, truthful answer to one question. Baobhan Sith only.

## Oberon's Abilities

> Storyteller reference only. Do not share with other players before the reveal.

**Public sheet** *(what other characters see him use)*

- **Sovereign's Mantle** *(Court — Presence)* — Declare himself the formal "host" of any conflict, setting the stakes for both sides.
- **Wanderer's Step** *(Wyrd — Craft)* — Enter any private scene uninvited; no one may refuse him entry.
- **Crown of Idleness** *(Court — Guile)* — Pass any direct question by saying "I have no opinion on this." Cannot be compelled to answer.

**Secret sheet** *(revealed when Oberon becomes Vortigern — see [storyteller/vortigern-reveal.md](../storyteller/vortigern-reveal.md))*

- **Vortigern's Heart** — On reveal, gain 5 additional stat points distributed freely across any stats. All prior oaths Oberon swore are void. No action point cost — this fires on the reveal itself.
- **Tyrant's Word** *(Wyrd — Sight)* — Cannot be lied to for the remainder of the evening.
- **The Long Hunger** *(Iron — Might)* — Each character you defeat in a conflict after the reveal: choose one of their abilities. You may use that ability once tonight by spending 1 action point as normal.

*Note: Oberon's abilities in his [private brief](../characters/named/oberon.md) are the player-facing versions. This is the design reference.*

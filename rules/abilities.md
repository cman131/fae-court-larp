# Abilities — *The Queen's Revel*

Abilities let you bend the rules in ways ordinary stat challenges cannot.

## How abilities work

**Buying ability uses at character creation.** Instead of picking a fixed list of abilities, you spend stat points on uses of specific abilities. Each use purchased gives you one activation you can spend during the evening. Abilities do not refresh — when your uses are gone, that ability is unavailable for the night.

**Cost per use.** Each ability has a tier that determines how many stat points you pay for one use:
- Tier 1 — 1 stat point per use
- Tier 2 — 2 stat points per use
- Tier 3 — 3 stat points per use

**Maximum 3 uses per ability.** You may not buy more than three uses of any single ability.

**Which pools you can buy from.** You may buy from the three universal pools (Iron, Wyrd, Glamour) and from your court's pool. Members of the Queen's Court (Knights) buy from any one of the three courts' pools in addition to universal.

**Cross-court unlock.** You may spend 2 stat points to unlock access to one ability from a court you don't belong to. Write the ability and its court in the blank on your character sheet. After unlocking, buy uses of it at the normal tier cost, still capped at 3.

**When you use an ability, narrate it.** Don't announce the ability name; describe what your character does. The mechanical effect happens regardless, but the storytelling is what the room remembers.

*Knight signature abilities are pre-loaded with 1 use before character creation and cannot be purchased for more — see [named-abilities.md](named-abilities.md). Oberon's player builds a standard sheet; his Vortigern abilities activate at the reveal and are not purchased.*

## Iron

*Physical force, endurance, and presence of body. Available to all.*

- **Steadfast** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — When you would lose a Mettle challenge, turn it into a tie instead.
- **Iron Stride** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Pass any one physical barrier (a barred entrance, a blocked path, a guarded door) without a challenge.
- **Unyielding** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — For the remainder of this scene, you cannot be expelled from it, physically moved within it, or compelled to act against your will by any ability.

---

## Wyrd

*Perception, magic, and the hidden currents of the fae. Available to all.*

- **Whispered Tongue** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Pass a short message silently to one person you can see. No challenge required.
- **Hearth's Mercy** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — You are unaffected by one ability just used against you this scene.
- **Trueseeing** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — Demand that one character answer one question fully and honestly. The question need not be yes/no.

---

## Glamour

*Charm, authority, and the bewitching weight of fae presence. Available to all.*

- **Iron Word** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — When you swear an oath aloud, you cannot break it without a Wyrd challenge at difficulty 5; in return, you cannot be magically compelled this scene.
- **Glamour-Strike** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Force one opponent to lose an RPS challenge without throwing.
- **Reverie** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — All characters within earshot become transfixed by you for 3 minutes; they cannot initiate challenges with one another while you hold their attention.

---

## Court of Storms

*(Aurora's court — members only, or cross-court unlock)*

- **Lure of the Revel** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Draw one character into a private conversation; they cannot disengage for 3 minutes without spending a Glamour point.
- **Stormwalk** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Pass any one social barrier (a closed circle, a forbidden room, a scene you weren't part of) without challenge.
- **Song of Aurora** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — While you sing, dance, or perform, any character within earshot must answer one personal question truthfully when you stop.

---

## Court of Beasts

*(Spriggan's court — members only, or cross-court unlock)*

- **Earth-Sense** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Detect when anyone in your line of sight is lying about a location, a movement, or a meeting.
- **Old Grudge** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Publicly name a wrong done to your clan. The accused must respond honestly or spend a Presence point to deflect.
- **Beast Form** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — When you would lose any physical challenge, turn it into a tie instead.

---

## Court of Mirrors

*(Mab's court — members only, or cross-court unlock)*

- **Maskwear** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Briefly impersonate another character's voice or manner. Convince one character of one small lie without a challenge.
- **Boggart Bargain** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Offer a secret-for-secret trade. If accepted, both parties must trade truthfully.
- **Hall of Mirrors** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — When you are the target of a challenge or ability, redirect it to another character within 3 paces instead.

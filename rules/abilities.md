# Abilities — *The Queen's Revel*

Abilities let you bend the rules in ways ordinary stat challenges cannot.

## Powers overview

Characters have two kinds of powers: **disciplines** (universal — available to all) and **court abilities** (specific to your court).

**Buying powers at character creation.** Spend Power points on discipline levels and court ability uses. See [rank-and-stats.md](rank-and-stats.md) for your Power point budget by rank.

**Discipline levels.** Each discipline has three levels. You must buy them in sequence (level 2 requires level 1). Cost: 1 Power point per level. Higher levels do not replace lower — you keep all powers you've unlocked.

**Court ability uses.** Court abilities use the "buy uses" model. Each use costs Power points equal to the ability's tier. Maximum 3 uses per ability.

| Tier | Cost per use |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 | 1 Power point |
| Tier 2 | 2 Power points |
| Tier 3 | 3 Power points |

**Which court pool you can buy from.** You may buy from your court's pool. Members of the Queen's Court (Knights) buy from any one of the three courts' pools in addition to disciplines.

**Cross-court unlock.** Spend 2 Power points to unlock access to one ability from a court you don't belong to. After unlocking, buy uses at the normal tier cost, still capped at 3.

**The Wyrd pool.** Each character has a Wyrd pool (fixed by rank — see [rank-and-stats.md](rank-and-stats.md)). Spend Wyrd to activate non-passive discipline powers. The Wyrd pool refreshes fully at the start of each session.

**When you use a power, narrate it.** Don't announce the power name; describe what your character does. The mechanical effect happens regardless, but the storytelling is what the room remembers.

*Knight signature abilities are pre-loaded with 1 use before character creation and cannot be purchased for more — see [named-abilities.md](named-abilities.md). Oberon's player builds a standard sheet; his Vortigern abilities activate at the reveal and are not purchased.*

## Disciplines (Universal)

*All five disciplines are available to every character regardless of court.*

---

### Imbas

*Prophetic fae sight — the gift of second sight, truth-reading, and far-seeing. Drawn from the Celtic imbas forosnai, the illumination between the hands.*

**Level 1** *(VtM Auspex 1 + 2)*
- **Passive:** Ignore darkness and concealment penalties. You cannot be surprised.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Read one target's emotional state and whether they are telling the truth. No challenge required — the impression simply comes.

**Level 2** *(VtM Auspex 3 + 4)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Touch a person or object to receive a vivid impression of the most emotionally significant event connected to it in the past day. Mental challenge — defender resists with Cunning.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Read a willing or unaware target's surface thoughts. Mental challenge if the target is aware and resisting — defender resists with Cunning.

**Level 3** *(VtM Auspex 5)*
- **Active (2 Wyrd):** Send your consciousness to any location you have visited. You observe it for up to 5 minutes. Your body stands inert and vulnerable while projecting.

---

### Geis

*The fae binding — commands that cannot be refused, memories rewritten, obligations that burn. Named for the Celtic sacred prohibition that cannot be broken without consequence.*

**Level 1** *(VtM Dominate 1 + 2)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Issue a command — either an immediate single action ("drop it", "leave", "kneel") or a post-hypnotic instruction that triggers on a stated condition later in the session. Social challenge — attacker uses Glamour or Guile, defender resists with Mettle. Requires eye contact.

**Level 2** *(VtM Dominate 3 + 4)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Erase or rewrite one target's memory of a specific event up to one hour long. Social challenge — attacker uses Guile, defender resists with Cunning. Changes are discovered if the target compares notes with a witness.

**Level 3** *(VtM Dominate 4 + 5)*
- **Active (2 Wyrd):** Lay a binding geis — a sworn obligation the target cannot violate for the rest of the session. State the geis aloud. Social challenge — attacker uses Glamour or Guile, defender resists with Mettle. A target may break a geis by spending 3 Wyrd; doing so is visible to all present.

---

### Veil

*The hidden glamour — the fae art of concealment, disguise, and making others forget. Like all glamour, it is beautiful and untrue.*

**Level 1** *(VtM Obfuscate 1 + 2)*
- **Passive:** Become invisible while standing completely still. Ends the moment you move or initiate a challenge.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Remain invisible while moving. Ends if you initiate a challenge or are physically touched.

**Level 2** *(VtM Obfuscate 3 + 4)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Assume the appearance and voice of any specific person you have met. Social challenge — attacker uses Glamour, defender resists with Sight — if a suspicious observer actively scrutinises you.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** After leaving a scene, cause one character who observed you to forget you were present. Mental challenge — attacker uses Guile, defender resists with Sight.

**Level 3** *(VtM Obfuscate 5)*
- **Active (2 Wyrd):** Extend your Veil to cover up to five willing characters within arm's reach. All benefit from your current level of concealment.

---

### Awen

*Sovereign fae magnetism — the overwhelming pull of divine inspiration that draws hearts or scatters them. Named for the Celtic concept of poetic and creative fire that moves through all living things.*

**Level 1** *(VtM Presence 1 + 2)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Project overwhelming awe or dread — your choice. Social challenge — attacker uses Glamour or Presence, against all targets in earshot who resist with Mettle. Those who fail are either drawn helplessly toward you (awe) or must immediately retreat from the scene (dread). They may return after 3 minutes.

**Level 2** *(VtM Presence 3 + 4)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Entrance one target into devoted attachment. Social challenge — attacker uses Glamour, defender resists with Cunning. For the rest of the scene they will not act against your interests without spending 2 Wyrd to overcome the effect.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Summon a specific character who is elsewhere in the venue. They feel a compulsion to seek you out and know your general location. They may resist by spending 2 Wyrd.

**Level 3** *(VtM Presence 5)*
- **Active (2 Wyrd):** Radiate majesty. No character may attack you, publicly oppose you, or use an ability against you while this lasts. Any character who wishes to do so must first spend 2 Wyrd to overcome it. Lasts until the end of the scene or until you initiate a challenge of your own.

---

### The Green

*The wild bond — the primal fae connection to beast and wilderness, to the living things that do not speak in courts but know everything.*

**Level 1** *(VtM Animalism 1 + 2)*
- **Passive:** Understand the intentions and recent impressions of any animal you observe. You know if an animal has been disturbed, used for observation, or set as a ward.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Call all animals in the area to you. They arrive within a few minutes and will not threaten you.

**Level 2** *(VtM Animalism 3 + 4)*
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Ride an animal's senses for up to 5 minutes. You perceive everything it perceives. Your body remains stationary and inattentive.
- **Active (1 Wyrd):** Rouse the primal beast in one character. They must win a Physical challenge — attacker uses Cunning, defender resists with Mettle — or act on raw instinct (aggression, flight, or hunger) on their next significant action.

**Level 3** *(VtM Animalism 5)*
- **Active (2 Wyrd):** Call the wild in force. A pack, swarm, or flock answers you and acts on your behalf for the scene. You may declare one Physical challenge per minute as coming from the beasts rather than yourself, and you cannot be physically expelled from the area while they are present.

## Court of Storms

*(Aurora's court — members only, or cross-court unlock)*

- **Lure of the Revel** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Draw one character into a private conversation; they cannot disengage for 3 minutes without spending a Glamour point.
- **Stormwalk** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Pass any one social barrier (a closed circle, a forbidden room, a scene you weren't part of) without challenge.
- **Song of Aurora** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — While you sing, dance, or perform, any character within earshot must answer one personal question truthfully when you stop.

---

## Court of Beasts

*(Woodwose's court — members only, or cross-court unlock)*

- **Earth-Sense** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Detect when anyone in your line of sight is lying about a location, a movement, or a meeting.
- **Old Grudge** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Publicly name a wrong done to your clan. The accused must respond honestly or spend a Presence point to deflect.
- **Beast Form** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — When you would lose any physical challenge, turn it into a tie instead.

---

## Court of Mirrors

*(Mab's court — members only, or cross-court unlock)*

- **Maskwear** *(Tier 1 — 1 pt/use)* — Briefly impersonate another character's voice or manner. Convince one character of one small lie without a challenge.
- **Boggart Bargain** *(Tier 2 — 2 pts/use)* — Offer a secret-for-secret trade. If accepted, both parties must trade truthfully.
- **Hall of Mirrors** *(Tier 3 — 3 pts/use)* — When you are the target of a challenge or ability, redirect it to another character within 3 paces instead.

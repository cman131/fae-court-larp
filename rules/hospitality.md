# Hospitality — the Rules of the Revel

Until Act IV begins, **the Queen's hospitality is binding**. Even though Morgan is not present, her standing word holds:

- **No physical violence.** Iron contests are allowed in the social-physical mode only — blocking a door, restraining a hand, intimidating with bulk, refusing to step aside. Drawn blades and blood spilled in the hall break hospitality and the breaker is subject to immediate Knight sanction.
- **Social and Wyrd conflicts are open.** Duel with words, illusions, riddles, bargains, and gossip all night.
- **Oaths sworn aloud are binding.** A toast or oath made in public, especially before a Knight, cannot be broken without a Wyrd 5+ conflict and probable consequences. Be careful what you say in front of witnesses.

When Act IV's hospitality breaks, anything goes. You'll know when it happens — the storyteller will announce it clearly, and the room's tone will shift.

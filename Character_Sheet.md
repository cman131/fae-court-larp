### Universal abilities *(anyone may take)*

- ☐ **Glamour-Strike** — 1/scene. Force one opponent to lose an RPS without throwing. Stat bid still happens.
- ☐ **Iron Word** — When you swear an oath aloud, you cannot break it without a Wyrd 5+ conflict. In return, you cannot be magically compelled this scene.
- ☐ **Whispered Tongue** — Pass a short message silently to one person you can see.
- ☐ **Hearth's Mercy** — 1/evening. Restore one used ability or 2 spent stat points.
- ☐ **Trueseeing** — 1/evening. Demand a yes/no question be answered honestly.

### Court of Storms *(Aurora's)*

- ☐ **Lure of the Revel** *(Glamour)* — Draw one character into a private conversation; they cannot disengage for 3 minutes without spending a Glamour point.
- ☐ **Stormwalk** *(Iron)* — 1/evening. Pass any one social barrier (closed circle, forbidden room) without challenge.
- ☐ **Song of Aurora** *(Wyrd)* — While you sing, dance, or perform, any character within earshot must answer one personal question truthfully when you stop.

### Court of Beasts *(Spriggan's)*

- ☐ **Beast Form** *(Iron)* — 1/evening. When you would lose a physical conflict, force a tie instead.
- ☐ **Earth-Sense** *(Wyrd)* — Detect when anyone in your line of sight is lying about a location, a movement, or a meeting.
- ☐ **Old Grudge** *(Glamour)* — Publicly name a wrong done to your clan. The accused must respond honestly or pay a Glamour point to deflect.

### Court of Mirrors *(Mab's)*

- ☐ **Maskwear** *(Glamour)* — Briefly impersonate another character's voice or manner. Convince one character of one small lie.
- ☐ **Boggart Bargain** *(Wyrd)* — Offer a secret-for-secret trade. If accepted, both parties must trade truthfully.
- ☐ **Hall of Mirrors** *(Iron)* — 1/evening. When attacked, redirect the attack to another character within 3 paces.

### Knight signature *(Knights only — add automatically for your title)*

- ☐ **Sun's Edge** *(Barghest)* — 1/evening. Win one Iron conflict without RPS.
- ☐ **Lake's Veil** *(Mélusine)* — 1/evening. Vanish from a scene for up to 5 real minutes; return anywhere in the play space.
- ☐ **Wind's Lament** *(Baobhan Sith)* — 1/evening. Compel one direct, truthful answer to one question.

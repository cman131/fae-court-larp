## Persona and Appearance

**Faction:** Court of Beasts (your court).

**Rank:** Sovereign (20 Attribute pts / 28 Skill pts / 8 Power pts; 3 tokens).

Massive, wolfman, visibly inhuman — the strongest hand-to-hand fighter at this Revel, and you carry that knowledge without arrogance; it is simply true. Wear at least two openly fae features that courtly Sidhe keep hidden: claws, a muzzle, fur, something that signals what you are the moment you enter the room. Move with the weight of something that has never needed to be subtle. Speak rarely, and when you do, with absolute certainty.

You carry a cursed demonic hunger that you have managed your entire life. Physical exertion keeps it leashed. Courtly stillness feeds it. The performance of dignity tonight costs you more than it costs anyone else in the room.

## Relationships

**One slot pre-set: Mab (Negative).**

An ancient blood debt. A century ago, Mab's Mirror Sidhe killed one of your kin by trickery, and no recompense has ever been paid. Walk over to Mab's player during the mixer and lock this in together — the grievance is cold and very old. Note: the challenge chain eventually requires you to face Mab or her champion, but she will set her own terms before she agrees to any duel. That is her right by ancient rule. Whatever she asks, you do it.

**One slot pre-set: Barghest (Negative).**

She was yours before she was Morgan's. You have carried this quietly for the years of her knighthood. Lock the slot in with her player at the mixer — present it as professional tension, an old soldier's rivalry. Let the real weight come out during play.

**Aurora — beyond the slot system.**

You love her. She does not notice. During the mixer, walk over to Aurora's player and establish the relationship together — frame it from your side as deep devotion and respect, from her side as genuine warmth without depth. It is not a negative. It is not quite a positive. It is simply the fact that shapes how you move through the room tonight.

**Strong positive slot: free.** Your deepest bonds are within your own court. Consider one of your Court of Beasts Sidhe as a strong positive — someone who knows about the hunger and helps you manage it quietly.

## Court Storyline: Justice for the Forest

*Storyteller reference: `storyteller/storylines/court-of-beasts/index.md`*

Three weeks ago, Barghest swept through the Autumn Forest and slaughtered numerous weak fae sheltering there. Everyone at this Revel has heard of it. No one knows why — was it a royal command, a loss of control, a provocation answered? Oberon reached out to you before the Revel, in his role as the Forest's grieving shepherd, and asked for justice. You intend to provide it.

To formally challenge a Knight to a duel, you must first work through every fae at this Revel in order — defeating each court's underlings before you may challenge their Sovereign or champion, earning Mab's terms before she will face you, and requiring the Queen herself to be present as witness for the final duel. Tonight you fight for the Forest. Tonight you find out the truth. And tonight, perhaps, you answer a question you have been carrying for years.

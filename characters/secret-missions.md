## Setup Instructions

These cards are for any player who is not Oberon, a Sovereign, or a Knight тАФ i.e., the Sidhe and Sprites of each court. At session zero, each such player draws **one Secret Mission** card. Sovereigns and Knights receive assigned secret goals in their briefs (see `characters/named/`); Oberon's secret play is entirely scripted.

Relationships are handled separately through the session-zero Relationship Mixer (see [storyteller/session-zero.md](../storyteller/session-zero.md)).

**Storyteller setup:** Print this file. Cut along the dotted lines. Fold each card once. Drop them in a container. Players draw in front of you so you can quietly note who drew what тАФ useful if a mission collides with a court storyline and needs a quiet re-draw.

A few cards reference "a Sovereign" or "a Knight" generically тАФ when drawn, the player picks which one fits their story, in soft consultation with the storyteller so it dovetails with the chosen character's existing plot.
